# APPSHEET SLICE SPEC

## HO_SO_ACTIVE
Source: HO_SO_MASTER
Condition: `[IS_DELETED] = FALSE`

## TASK_OPEN
Source: TASK_MAIN
Condition: `IN([STATUS]; LIST("NEW"; "ASSIGNED"; "IN_PROGRESS"; "WAITING"))`

## TASK_DONE
Source: TASK_MAIN
Condition: `[STATUS] = "DONE"`

## FIN_DRAFT
Source: FINANCE_TRANSACTION
Condition: `[STATUS] = "NEW"`

## FIN_CONFIRMED
Source: FINANCE_TRANSACTION
Condition: `[STATUS] = "CONFIRMED"`
